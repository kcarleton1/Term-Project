Asia

China

  • Chimelong Safari Park-panda sanctuary with other native animals
  • Nanshan Temple-monument surrounded by the ocean, important tourist spot
  • Tian Tan Buddha-temple signifying harmony between man and nature
  • The Bund-neo-classical buildings along the Huang Pu River

Thailand

  • Elephant Rescue Park-sanctuary devoted to protecting animals
  • The Sanctuary of Truth-wooden construction
  • Soi Dog Foundation-rescue organization devoted to caring for street dogs and cats
  • Bamboo Island-island off the coast where you can enjoy the sandy beaches and crystal waters

Japan

  • Universal Studios Japan-amusement park for all your favorite characters
  • Toyota Commemorative Museum of Industry & Technology-a museum about the evolution of the Toyota franchise
  • Uminonakamichi Seaside Park- a leisure park with seasonal flowers
  • Nikko Tosho-gu-a shrine in honor of Tokugawa Ieyasu in the heart of a forest

Malaysia

  • Legoland Malaysia-an amusement park for Lego characters
  • Malaka Straits Mosque-a religious destination
  • Tanjung Aru Beach-a relaxing place by the water
  • Semenggoh Nature Reserve-a sanctuary filled with wildlife

Vietnam

  • Old Quarter-a shopping paradise comprised of 40 streets intersecting
  • Marble Mountains-little caves filled with ancient sculptures
  • Hoi An Ancient Town-a historic town that's a staple for tourism
  • Halong Bay-an inlet with crystal blue waters perfect for kayaking and swimming
