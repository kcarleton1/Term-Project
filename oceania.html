Australia/Oceania

New Zealand

  • SkyTower-a tall tower overlooking downtown Auckland
  • Christchurch Botanic Gardens-beautiful floral and a sanctuary to indulge the scenery
  • Wai-O-Tapu Thermal Wonderland-geysers created from the Earth and becomes a surreal place to visit
  • Museum of New Zealand Te Papa Tongarewa-museum of New Zealand's history

Fiji

  • Sabeto Hot Springs-warm and inviting place to swim
  • Garden of The Sleeping Giant-garden filled with 2000 types of floral to enjoy
  • Big Bula Water Park-amusement park on the ocean
  • Fiji Culture Village-community built on Fiji cultures and rituals that were created centuries ago

Papua New Guinea

  • Kokoda Track-a hike that takes you among nature
  • Mount Tavurvur-a tour that brings you along famous volancos and hot springs
  • Mount Wilhelm-a hike that takes you up the highest mountain
  • Turnbull War Memorial Park-a monument commorating a major war

French Polynesia

  • Matira Beach-a relaxing beach by the water
  • Belvedere Lookout-a picturesque photo opportunity among nature
  • Bora Bora Turtle Center-a facility committed to taking care of turtles
  • Maraa Grotto-an oasis built within the waters of nature

Solomon Islands

  • Kennedy Island- a small inhabited island named after JFK
  • Bonegi 1 & 2-a coral reef filled with unique fish and plants
  • Shark Point-a dive site to swim with sharks
  • Japanese War Memorial-a place devoted to commemorate a piece of history
