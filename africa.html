Africa

South Africa

  • Table Mountain Aerial Cableway-adventure around Cape Town in a cable car above the sky!
  • Rietvlei Nature Reserve-a sanctuary filled with Africa's lovely natural landscape
  • Chapman's Peak Drive-a lookout onto the crystal waters of the African coast
  • Granschhoek Motor Museum-a museum filled with historic automobiles over the years

Egypt

  • Ras Mohammed National Park-a natural oasis filled with different rivers and streams for visitors to enjoy
  • Pyramids of Giza-a staple piece in the heart of Egypt and a must see!
  • Valley of the Kings-ancient burial ground filled with Egyptian hieroglyphics and architecture
  • Museum of Egyptian Antiquities-a historic museum that highlights artifacts dating centuries back

Ethiopia

  • Holy Trinity Cathedral-a religious temple honoring important figures of Ethiopia
  • Rock-Hewn Churches of Lalibela-ancient ruins and tunnels built out of red volcanic rock
  • Mount Entoto- a beautiful lookout spot
  • Meskel Square-massive shopping center for tourists

Tanzania

  • Nakupenda Beach Nature Reserve-location to relax along the crystal waters of the coast
  • Stone Town-town square with beautiful cathedrals and markets
  • Cheetah's Rock-a cheetah reservation center driven to protecting wildlife
  • Materuni Waterfalls-beautiufl waterfall to enjoy

Madagascar

  • Lemur's Park-lemur sanctuary filled with perservation of wildlife
  • Lokobe Nature Special Reserve-conservation land devoted to plants and animals
  • Tsingy Rouge Park-amazing red canyons to explore
  • Ankarafantsika National Park-conservation refuge spot for lemurs and native birds
